About Me:

HI! I'm Tomasz, a beginner self-thought front-end developer. Currently working as a CNC Programmer with almost 10 years of experience in this field. I always had a passion and a knack for automation, making things simpler and more user friendly. I've turned my attention towards IT and front-end as it is evolving, a fast-developing sector which gives a lot of opportunities to learn and grow! 

Currently, I'm learning from online courses and building small projects to hone my skills and knowledge. You can see some of them in the Projects section.

In my free time, I like to be active! I spend a lot of time working out, hiking and attending dance classes. I'm also very dedicated to self-improvement, always looking to make myself a little bit better than a day before, which also translates to my work-life.
